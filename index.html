About Me

My first IT job experience was at Hewlett-Packard in Ireland in 2015. I worked as a German Technical Support Analyst, providing first-line technical support on the full range of hardware and software. My daily tasks were to deliver solutions through problem research and analytical troubleshooting. Later, I spent a few years in taxation, implementing new automated tax services in compliance with legal requirements and cooperating with software developers. These prior work experiences made me detail-oriented and guided me to discover new work methods and technologies.

Finally, my passion for IT got me back on track. I decided to dive into the World of Coding and completed a 12-week intense Full-Stack Web Development course in November 2021. I learned about widely used programming languages and frameworks like JavaScript, PHP and React.js. Since I felt fascinated by the UI design and the code that powers it in an application, I have chosen to focus on front-end development. Eventually, I enjoyed it more coding with React.js and its UI-driven layouts.

After all, I started my first internship as a Junior Front-End Developer in May 2022 to gain experience in supporting software development. The project was to build and host an internal order processing software on the Google Cloud Platform for a digital marketing company. I assisted by creating and maintaining front-end code in React.js (TypeScript) and cooperating with developer colleagues via GitLab and Asana. My job was, among others, optimising the user interface design and customising user-facing components to meet operational needs.

I am currently working as a User Acceptance Testing Specialist analysing and testing specific user stories in collateral management services inside a Business Acceptance Testing environment with an agile methodology approach. I execute test scenarios on the Xact Web Portal application and simulate the client behaviour by sending different SWIFT messages in XML format to the software. Nevertheless, I create test documentation proving that system changes and improvements are appropriately delivered.

To expand the scope of my coding skills, I am always open to discussing and joining challenging front-end projects
